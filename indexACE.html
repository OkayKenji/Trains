GTFS (General Transit Feed Specification)

A GTFS feed is composed of a series of text files collected in a ZIP file. Each file models a particular aspect of transit information: stops, routes, trips, and other schedule data. The data can be used to power trip planners, time table publishers, and a variety of applications, that use public transit information in some way.
